About Us

For generations, Aling Conching Bakery’s Canteen has been a beloved local spot for freshly baked goods and home-cooked meals. Founded with a passion for traditional Filipino flavors, we pride ourselves on using high-quality ingredients to bring you the best bread, pastries, and dishes. Whether you’re craving a hot pandesal in the morning or a hearty meal for lunch, we have something for everyone!
